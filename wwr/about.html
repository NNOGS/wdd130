NNOGS WHITEWATER RAFTING COMPANY

Here in NNOGS WHITEWATER RAFTING COMPANY, below are all we stand for:
Purpose: To give our customers an experience not found anywhere else.
Mission: To grant our customers an alternative to boredom.
Creed: Learn the ropes while you glide — none was born to do it better without experience.
Motto: Never Let The Tides Win

History

This company started in 2012 with only two people to pilot it. It was a challenge back then, as we started with just two air-pump boats and no one to help with navigation. Taxes were a challenge until we broke even, but soon we witnessed massive patronage, which got us out of debt.

It didn’t end there. What started out as a struggle turned into a great adventure, because we have been learning on the job day by day. From a wooden raft to rubber, and now a balloon raft, we are still heading toward sophistication. To be candid, it can only be God.
