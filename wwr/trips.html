Our Most Popular Rafting Trips

Family Float Adventure

Enjoy a calm and scenic float down the gentle parts of the river. Perfect for beginners and families who want a relaxing day on the water surrounded by nature’s beauty.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Minimum Age: 5+

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Experience the thrill of powerful class IV and V rapids in this adrenaline-pumping adventure. Our expert guides ensure safety as you conquer the wildest stretches of the river.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Minimum Age: 16+

Sunset Scenic Expedition

Float through calm evening waters as the sun sets over the canyon. A perfect mix of adventure and serenity for couples or small groups looking for something magical.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Minimum Age: 10+
